SAP Citywest Public Transport

Public transport information about getting to and from the SAP office in Dublin.

View the Project on GitHub zky829/SAPCitywestPublicTransport

What is this page for?

This page documents the modes of transport to and from SAP Citywest. There isn't much detailed information on the Citywest Campus site, and of course the SAP Employee Bus is not mentioned there as it's SAP-specific.

Ways to get to and from SAP Citywest, Dublin 24:

  • Dublin Bus
  • Luas
  • Citywest Luas shuttle bus
  • SAP Bus
  • Citywest D4 bus

SAP Bus

This bus is for SAP employees. It runs twice a day - once in the morning starting at O'Connell Bridge, going along Aston Quay then through Inchicore to the SAP main building and AppHaus, and once in the evening going back the same way along Ormond Quay.

Schedule & Stops

To SAP

08:15: Leaves outside the Londis at O'Connell Bridge.

It stops along Aston Quay at the Dublin Bus stops. The next stop is outside the Smock Alley Theatre at 08:18

From SAP

17:45: Leaves outside main building.

A few minutes after 17:45 (depending on traffic): Leaves outside AppHaus - location.

Description

A single ticket costs €1.50, and can be purchased in the Costa cafés of either the main building or the AppHaus.

Description

This bus is infrequent (around every hour), and takes a long time compared to the other modes of transport, as it goes on a tour of West Dublin before properly heading towards town.

Luas

The Red Line will take you to the Citywest Campus stop. From there, SAP is a 10-minute walk. A Luas shuttle bus operates in the morning and evening, leaving from the Luas stop and stopping at various companies.
